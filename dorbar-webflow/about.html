About Our Firm

Decades of Experience. A Legacy of Trust.

Founded with a commitment to excellence and integrity, Sterling & Associates has been guiding clients through complex legal matters for over two decades, building lasting relationships based on results and respect.

Meet Our Attorneys

Who We Are

At Dorbar & Associates, we believe that effective legal representation requires not only expertise but also empathy. Our team of experienced attorneys provides individualized attention to every client, combining strategic insight with personal commitment. We don't just handle cases—we build partnerships built on trust, transparency, and a shared commitment to achieving your goals.

Our Mission

To deliver unparalleled legal guidance with honesty and precision, ensuring every client receives the strategic counsel they need to protect their interests and achieve their objectives.

Our Vision

To redefine modern legal representation through trust and transparency, setting the standard for client-focused service and establishing ourselves as the premier boutique law firm.

Our Values

Integrity in every interaction. Compassion for every client. Commitment to excellence in every case. These principles guide our work and define our firm's character.

Our Journey Through the Years

2005

Sterling & Associates established in New York City with a focus on corporate and family law.

2015

Recognized by Legal 500 as a top-tier regional law firm, marking our emergence as industry leaders.

2025

Now serving 500+ clients nationwide with a team of 15 attorneys and continued recognition for excellence.
